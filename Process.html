Stepping Stones

How It Works

Follow these simple steps to secure financial backing and launch or expand your business idea on EmpowerHer.

1

Login or Sign Up

Create your personal profile on the EmpowerHer portal. Select whether you are an entrepreneur applying for funds or an investor searching for projects.

2

Enter Profile Details

Fill in details about your business sector, age, location, and the amount of funding needed to get started.

3

Verify Your Identity

Upload KYC documents (ID proofs, business registrations) through our fully secure document upload utility.

4

Propose Your Business Idea

Publish details of your venture. Upload a brief video pitch so that investors get to see the passion and idea behind your proposal.

5

Review by Investors

Once verified by the platform, your pitch becomes visible to registered investors who specialize in supporting microenterprises.

6

Discuss & Negotiate

Connect directly with interested investors to discuss terms, support, and repayment schedule options.

7

Receive Disbursed Funds

Once finalized, the microloan is sent directly to your verified bank account via our payment framework.

8

Flexible Repayment

Make online repayments through our schedule manager. Split monthly payments into smaller weekly parts to match your cash flow.

9

Share Your Success Story

Publish your progress on the stories board to inspire other women entrepreneurs across India.
